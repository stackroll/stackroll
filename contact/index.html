FAQ

Common
questions.

How much does a project cost?

Depends on scope. A marketing website starts around €1,000. Complex web apps with backends can range from €5,000–€15,000+. We always give a fixed quote upfront.

How long does a project take?

Simple websites: 2–4 weeks. Full-stack applications: 1–3 months. We'll give you a realistic timeline before starting.

What technologies do you use?

We pick the best tool for the job. Our core stack includes C++, Java, Python, Go, JavaScript/TypeScript, Spring Boot, React, and Docker.

Do I own the code?

Yes. 100%. Everything we write belongs to you. Full source code, no licensing restrictions, no lock-in.

Can you work with my existing system?

Absolutely. We can integrate with existing backends, APIs, databases, or extend what you already have.

What happens after launch?

We include post-launch support. Bugs, tweaks, questions — we don't disappear. Ongoing maintenance plans are available too.
